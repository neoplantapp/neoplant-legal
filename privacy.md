# NeoPlant Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

NeoPlant (“we”, “us”) provides a plant tracking app that helps you manage care schedules and reminders.

## Data we collect
- Account information you provide (such as email)
- App data you create (your plants, care actions, streak status)
- Device information needed to operate the app (e.g., push notification token)

## How we use data
- To provide and improve the app
- To sync your data across devices
- To send care reminders if you enable notifications
- To provide subscription access if you purchase Pro

## Sharing
We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers needed to operate the app (e.g., hosting/authentication, notifications, payments).

## Subscriptions
Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment details.

## Your choices
You can delete plants and log data inside the app. You can request account deletion by contacting us.
